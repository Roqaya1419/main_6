What is Coding Dojo?

Coding Dojo is a global technology education company that offers a three-full-stack coding bootcamp, full-stack part-time programs, as well as courses on data science and other emerging technologies. Our mission is to train students to become self-sufficient tech professionals, support underserved communities, and increase diversity in the tech industry. At Coding Dojo we pride ourselves on possessing a culture of execution that caters to wave makers rather than wave riders. If you’re interested in joining a diverse and dynamic team who value speed, continuous improvement, measurement, focus, accountability, and always attacking the gap, you’ll feel right at home.
